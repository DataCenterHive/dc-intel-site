Data Quality & Methodology

Our platform automates data collection from municipal permit systems using purpose-built scrapers for each jurisdiction. Daily refresh cycles ensure you receive the latest filings, approvals, and status changes.

All data undergoes automated deduplication, standardization, and enrichment before delivery. We validate project addresses, normalize contractor names, and flag potential data center projects based on valuation thresholds, square footage, and permit descriptions.

Historical data is preserved with full audit trails, allowing trend analysis and year-over-year comparisons across markets.
