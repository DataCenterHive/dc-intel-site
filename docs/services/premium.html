Frequently Asked Questions

How fast are the "real-time" alerts?
Premium alerts are delivered within 6-12 hours of permit filing (vs. weekly digests in Basic). We check high-priority cities twice daily and send immediate email alerts when new DC permits match your filters.
What does AI-powered research summaries include?
Our AI reads each permit and extracts: project size (sq ft), power capacity (MW), cooling methods, estimated cost, completion timeline, and operator identification. It also flags keywords like "data center," "hyperscale," "colocation," etc.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Premium mid-year?
Yes! We'll pro-rate your remaining Basic subscription value and apply it toward Premium. Most customers upgrade within 3-6 months after seeing the value of Basic tier.
What's included in the custom briefing?
One 20-30 page analyst-written report per year on a topic of your choice: specific market deep-dive, operator competitive analysis, regional trend report, or custom research question. Typical turnaround: 2-3 weeks.
How many custom dashboards can I create?
Unlimited! Create separate dashboards for different markets, operators, or use cases. Share with team members. Save custom filters for quick access.
When should I consider Enterprise instead?
Upgrade to Enterprise if you need: (1) Full API access for system integrations, (2) Unlimited custom briefings, (3) Direct analyst access (phone/video calls), (4) More than 10 user seats, or (5) Custom feature development.
