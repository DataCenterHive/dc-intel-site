Frequently Asked Questions

What does "unlimited custom briefings" really mean?
No hard limits. Most Enterprise customers request 4-10 custom reports per year. Each briefing is 20-30 pages, analyst-written, tailored to your specific question. Typical topics: market deep-dives, operator competitive analysis, site selection research, M&A due diligence. Turnaround time: 1-2 weeks for standard briefings, faster for urgent requests.
How does the dedicated account manager work?
You'll have a single point of contact who understands your business, coordinates with our analysts and engineers, and ensures you're maximizing platform value. Typical cadence: weekly check-ins (first 3 months), then monthly. Available via phone, email, Slack, or Teams.
Can you integrate with our existing tools (Salesforce, Tableau, etc.)?
Yes. Our API is REST-based and works with any system that can consume JSON. We provide SDK libraries for Python and JavaScript. For complex integrations (Salesforce, Tableau, custom internal tools), we offer implementation support as part of your onboarding.
What's included in on-site training?
1-2 day workshop at your office (travel expenses included within contiguous U.S.). Day 1: Platform overview, best practices, advanced features. Day 2: Hands-on training with your actual use cases, custom dashboard setup, API integration guidance. Includes post-training support for 30 days.
How do SLA guarantees work?
99.9% uptime guarantee (less than 9 hours downtime/year). 4-hour response time for critical issues. Dedicated Slack/Teams channel for urgent requests. Monthly uptime reports. If we fail SLA targets, you receive service credits (pro-rated refund for downtime).
Is pricing negotiable for multi-year contracts?
Yes. We offer discounts for 2-year (10%) and 3-year (15%) commitments. Enterprise contracts are customized based on scope: number of users, API call volume, custom feature development, analyst hours needed. Contact us for a custom quote.
