Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the data updated?
Permit data is refreshed monthly (first week of each month). News feed updates daily. We scrape city permit portals on a rolling basis to capture new filings within 30 days.
Can I export the data?
Yes! You can export permit data, site directories, and reports to Excel or CSV format. No API access in the Basic tier, but manual exports are unlimited.
What's the difference vs. Premium?
Premium includes AI-powered research summaries, priority real-time alerts, advanced dashboards with custom filters, and 1 custom briefing per year. Best for mid-size firms needing more frequent updates and analyst support.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with limited access (5 users, basic features). No credit card required. Contact us to start your trial.
Can I upgrade to Premium later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime with pro-rated pricing. We'll credit your remaining Basic subscription value toward the Premium annual fee.
How many user seats are included?
The Basic tier includes 3 user seats. Need more? Additional seats are $1,250/year each, or consider upgrading to Premium (10 seats) or Enterprise (unlimited).
