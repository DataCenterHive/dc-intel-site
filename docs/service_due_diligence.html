Our Advisory Process

1. Market Positioning Assessment

  • Competitive Landscape Mapping: Identify all existing facilities and active projects within 10-mile radius of target asset
  • Operator Presence Analysis: Track major operators' expansion strategies and market share in the region
  • Supply/Demand Modeling: Estimate current capacity, utilization rates, and absorption forecasts based on permit data
  • Market Saturation Risk: Quantify competitive intensity and identify potential oversupply concerns

2. Growth Potential Evaluation

  • Historical Permit Trends: 5+ years of construction activity data revealing market maturity and growth cycles
  • Infrastructure Capacity: Utility upgrade patterns, substation additions, and fiber deployments indicating expansion runway
  • Expansion Feasibility: Land availability, zoning constraints, and utility capacity for on-site growth
  • Market Entry Timing: Optimal windows for capacity additions based on competitive dynamics

3. Competitive Intelligence

  • Pipeline Projects: Real-time tracking of competitor expansion plans before public announcements
  • Permitting Velocity: Speed from permit filing to certificate of occupancy indicates market momentum

  • Operator Strategies: Identify which operators are entering/exiting markets and why

4. Strategic Recommendations

  • Acquisition Pricing Guidance: Data-driven valuation ranges based on market comparables and competitive positioning
  • Risk Assessment: Market-specific risks (oversupply, competitive pressure, regulatory changes) with mitigation strategies
  • Post-Acquisition Strategy: Recommended capacity additions, market positioning, and competitive responses
  • Negotiation Leverage Points: Data-backed insights to support pricing discussions and deal terms
