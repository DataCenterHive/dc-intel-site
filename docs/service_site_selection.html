Our Methodology

1. Market Opportunity Assessment

  • Permit Activity Trends: Historical construction volume, growth trajectories, and capacity absorption rates based on actual permit filings
  • Operator Presence Analysis: Who's building where, expansion patterns, market entry timing, and facility counts by operator
  • Competitive Density Mapping: Heatmaps showing geographic concentration of existing facilities and active projects
  • Supply/Demand Indicators: Current capacity estimates, pipeline inventory, and absorption forecasts

2. Growth Trajectory Forecasting

  • Historical Patterns: 5+ years of permit data revealing market maturity and growth cycles
  • Valuation Trends: Average project values, investment intensity, and capital deployment patterns
  • Permitting Velocity: Speed from permit filing to certificate of occupancy indicates market momentum
  • Infrastructure Expansion: Utility upgrades, substation additions, and fiber deployments signal capacity for growth

3. Strategic Recommendations

  • Ranked Market Priorities: Top 3-5 markets scored across multiple criteria with supporting rationale
  • Competitive Positioning: Where to locate relative to existing operators and announced projects
  • Timing Guidance: Optimal market entry windows based on competitive dynamics and infrastructure readiness
  • Risk Mitigation: Market-specific risks (regulatory, environmental, competitive) and mitigation strategies
